What is DiversityDashboard?

The Diversity Dashboard enables you to track the activities of your diversity program including diversity events, diversity training, diversity council management, employee resource groups and alignment to corporate goals.

The diversity dashboard empowers diversity professionals to invest their resources more effectively, driving innovation and the creation of a more diverse and inclusive workplace. Over 80% of diversity professionals surveyed reported that tracking, measuring and analyzing diversity activities and programs is an issue in their organization.

Benefits of using DiversityDashboard

The Diversity Dashboard measures program effectiveness and performance. This shows how diversity is contributing towards achieving organizational goals. This helps to facilitate organizational culture change, creating a more diverse and inclusive workplace that engages employees and which leverages diversity for innovation.Only 9% of diversity professionals surveyed were satisfied with their current ability to measure diversity metrics.

Analysis of the metrics within the diversity dashboard provides insights into the return on investment for both budget spend and human capital. 75% of survey respondents reported that senior leaders wanted diversity metrics.
